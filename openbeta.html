Open Beta

Open Beta is a new feature that lets Midgets test games for people who have them in workings, at one point, there will be a screen, with the title and author of the game, and Midgets can play and leave a review telling them what to add, change, or remove. Sometimes the game will be run on a home computer, so sometimes only one can play at a time.

In short terms, Open Beta gives Midgets access to games made by members months (or days) before the public does.
