Section Blocks and Lockdown

Section Blocks are when you can lock certain sections of the website if they are deemed unsafe. This is a danger zone in the MM, so proceed with caution, website lockdowns can also be done here, where all sections deemed unnecessary for moderators are shut down. Sections include: Login, Signup, Info and similar pages, Messages and similar pages, Forgot Password, and courtroom. Only mod tools are left
