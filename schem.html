Pencil proj. schematic

Take a mechanical pencil, and then take off the tip of the pencil. Take a pair of scissors and cut off the bigger part. Then you are done. Just launch the inside of the pencil whenever you feel is neccessary, you will have trouble reloading due to the fact the spring is difficult to find

Uses: Launch it at ISG (jk), Send small notes via the inside of the projectile (pretty small note, needs to be taken out with lead later), Block incoming objects towards you, like battle balls and other things (Needs precise accuracy).

Difficulty: Easy, can be difficult to find the spring once launched. You can do a similar trick with a normal pencil and flexible jacket. More makeshift but easier to find
